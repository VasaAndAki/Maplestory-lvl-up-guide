Maple Story leveling guide (lvl 10-140)

First Job

  • lvl 10-20: Golems
  • lvl 20-30: Evil eyes

Second Job

  • lvl 30-45: Gold beach theme dungeon
  • lvl 45-55: Reina Strait theme dungeon
  • lvl 55-60: OPTIONAL:Ellinia Fairy Acadamy theme dungeon
    • Do this theme dungeon for early eye accessory
  • lvl 55-60: Possibly Evil Seals at Reina Strait theme dungeon

Third Job

  • lvl 60-68: Drakes
  • lvl 68-77: Orbis: Stairway to the sky 1
  • lvl 78-85: El Nath: Ice Valley 2
  • lvl 85-93: Arient: Sahel 2
  • lvl 93-97: Magaria: Underground research lab C-1
  • lvl 97-100: Dual Rash Beetles

Fourth Job

  • lvl 100-105: BOSS:Normal Zakum
  • lvl 105-110: Dual Rash Beetles
  • lvl 110-120: Robos(room 1-6)
  • lvl 120-128: Thanatos
  • lvl 128-133: Moon Bunnies
  • lvl 133-136: Jr Bains
  • lvl 136-140: Bains

This is the training guide I use to level up all my legion mules for level 2 link skill and level 2 legion buffs. Make sure to note that the order you do the theme dungeons in matters.
